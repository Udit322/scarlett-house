Our Story

Where Bombay
Finds Its Comfort

Scarlett House Bombay is more than a restaurant — it's a feeling. Tucked in the vibrant lanes of Bandra West, we've crafted a space where the city's energy meets the warmth of home-cooked comfort food.

From hand-selected wines and artisanal cheese boards to cocktails shaken with stories, every visit is an experience woven with care, flavour, and the unmistakable soul of Bombay.
